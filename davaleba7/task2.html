Dungeon Fighter (Neople)
Dungeon Fighter Online is a PC-based 2D side-scrolling arcade hack-n-slash game developed by Neople. In the game you assume the character of the main protagonist as you slash your way through hordes of minions and engage in epic boss battles.While the game follows the classic 2D side-scrolling format, it offers gamers a breath of fresh air and a welcome break from the array of mainstream online 3D and multidimensional games out there. And the fact that the game raked in more than a billion (US$ 1.052 billion) in revenues in 2015 speaks for itself. This earns Dungeon Fighter the third spot in the 10 Highest Grossing Online PC Games of 2015.
